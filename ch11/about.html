About Us

The Rainbow Gallery is owned and managed by the portrtait painter Suzanne Rembrandt. She specialises in paintings by local artists as well as her own work.

Suzanne is renowned for her portrait paintings and has received numerous commissions to paint many well known people.

 

Suzanne also stages exhibitions of her own and local artists' work from time to time. This can include pottery and sculpture, but paintings always predominate.

The Gallery was established in 1936 and was purchased by Suzanne in 1970. Suzanne is also a skilled picture framer.
